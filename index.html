Autoseed Systems

A new generation of simple, beautiful, and fully autonomous gardens.

Card 1

Gardening is hard.

  • It's time-consuming.
  • It requires constant knowledge and effort.
  • Watering, weeding, and pests are a daily battle.
  • Result: Many people who want to grow their own food, don't.

Card 2

A garden that grows itself.

  • Autoseed Systems is building fully autonomous, "set it and forget it" gardening systems.
  • We're designing for everyone: from the apartment balcony to the backyard.
  • Our systems handle the watering, the light, and the nutrients automatically.

Card 3

Grow your own food. With zero effort.

  • Our goal is to make it possible for anyone to grow their own fresh, healthy food.
  • We believe in simplicity, beauty, and open-source technology.
  • This is about food independence, sustainability, and quality of life.

Card 4

Open, Smart, & Simple

  • Open-Source: All our designs and code will be available for free.
  • 3D-Printable: Built with accessible parts that you can make and modify yourself.
  • Smart Systems: Using modern sensors and automation to give plants exactly what they need.
  • Inspired by the open-source FarmBot project.

Card 5

The "Johnny Autoseed" Project

  • "Johnny Autoseed" is the YouTube channel documenting this entire project.
  • This isn't just a faceless company; it's a real-world maker journey.
  • The channel will share everything: design, tutorials, build logs, files, and time-lapses of plants growing themselves.

Card 6

Technology that feels natural.

  • We are blending high-tech automation with an organic, minimalist design.
  • Our systems are designed to be beautiful, not just functional.
  • We use an earthy color palette and natural-feeling materials.
  • Technology should feel like a part of your home, not an intrusion.

Card 7

Beyond the planter.

  • Our work is the first step toward a future of local, automated food production.
  • Imagine backyard robotics and autonomous systems tending to neighborhood gardens.
  • This is about building resilient communities and a more sustainable food future.
